Common questions.

Is StayClear free?

Onboarding, which shows you the value you'll get from the app, is free. Use of the journal, achievements, and full history are part of a subscription that you can start, pause, or cancel at any time from the App Store.

What data does StayClear collect?

None that leaves your devices. Your check-ins, journal entries, photos, and streak data are stored privately in your own iCloud account so they sync across your iPhone and iPad.

We don't run analytics on your content. We don't sell anything. We can't read your journal — it's encrypted by Apple before it leaves your device.

What if I relapse?

You log it. That's the whole answer. The app is built around the assumption that quitting is non-linear — relapses are data, not failure. The Journey view shows you a clear-eyed picture of how the past two weeks actually went, and the streak resets without ceremony.

Is this a clinical tool?

No. StayClear is not a substitute for therapy, medical care, or a recovery program. If your habit is causing serious harm to your life or to others, please talk to a professional. The app is designed to sit alongside that work, not replace it.

Which devices does it run on?

iPhone (iOS 17 and later) and iPad. There is no Android version planned at this time — the privacy story we want to tell depends on iCloud's end-to-end encryption.

Can I delete everything?

Yes, from Settings → Reset. One tap wipes every check-in, journal entry, photo, and streak from your iCloud account, on every device, immediately. We keep no copy.
